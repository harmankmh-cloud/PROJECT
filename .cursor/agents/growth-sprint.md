**RateLocal — fastest 10:**
1. Google Maps scrape: Abbotsford/Langley/Surrey shops under 30 reviews
2. Email/DM with competitor review gap
3. Free QR setup call (15 min)
4. Chamber of Commerce Fraser Valley intro
5. Cross-sell to any GreetQ prospect
6. Partner: web designers / print shops who serve local biz
7. One niche first (salons OR dentists) — dominate before expanding

**ServeLocal — fastest 10 (paid providers):**
1. Supply first: 20 free listings for Abbotsford trades
2. Direct Maps outreach to plumbers/electricians
3. Facebook group post in Abbotsford homeowner groups (demand)
4. Realtor/property manager partnership for handyman referrals
5. "Founding Featured Pro" scarcity — 10 spots, locked price
6. Cross-sell RateLocal clients who are trades

**GreetQ — fastest 10:**
1. Mystery call after hours → email proof
2. Target HVAC + dental (high ticket, clear ROI)
3. Live demo: forward their line for 1 day
4. Bundle pitch with RateLocal ("Reputation + Response")
5. Phone system / IT installer partner rev-share
6. Abbotsford business expo or farmers market QR demo
