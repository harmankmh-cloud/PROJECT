# ServeLocal — Sales battlecard

**One-liner:** BC trades directory — free listing, $29/mo Featured, no pay-per-lead fees.

## Pricing

| Tier | Price | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Free listing | $0 | Basic profile, call-direct |
| Featured | **$29/mo** founding | Priority placement, job alerts |
| Premium | Waitlist | Not open checkout yet |

Thumbtack: **$15–80 per lead**. HomeStars: paid tiers + national competition.

## When to pitch

- Fraser Valley tradie tired of shared leads
- Wants visibility without Angi/Thumbtack lock-in
- Already known                — Abbotsford, Langley, Chilliwack corridor

## vs HomeStars

| | ServeLocal | HomeStars |
|--|------------|-----------|
| Geography | 12 BC cities (hyper-local) | National, Angi-owned |
| Cost model | Flat subscription | Tiered + competition |
| Lead type | Call direct + job post | Directory + quotes |
| Density | Early — honest | Thousands per metro |

**Talk track:** "HomeStars wins on volume. We win if you want Abbotsford homeowners to find *you* without bidding on every lead."

## vs Thumbtack

| | ServeLocal | Thumbtack |
|--|------------|-----------|
| Lead fees | None (subscription) | Pay per lead |
| Commitment | Cancel anytime | Credit packs |

## vs Jobbr / RealCraft

They do quotes + escrow at national scale. ServeLocal is **directory + direct contact** first — simpler, cheaper.

## Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "No leads yet" | Honest — density building in Fraser Valley. Featured = early placement |
| "Why not Google?" | SEO city × trade pages; you show up in ServeLocal search + cost guides |
| "Escrow?" | Job posts + Stripe booking partial; full escrow not the first wedge |

## Proof gaps

- Network effects 1/10 until 50+ pros in one corridor
- Don't claim Canada-wide dominance — **Abbotsford + Langley, 2 trades**
